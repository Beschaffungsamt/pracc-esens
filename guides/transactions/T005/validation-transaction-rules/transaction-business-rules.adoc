
= Business rules


The below listed business rules are additional rules added by the e-SENS pilot project, to ensure valid instance documents, and to ensure that identifiers needed in the PEPPOL Transport infrastructure are present.

[IMPORTANT]
====
Additional business rules are listed in the CEN BII documents, and these cannot be violated if the instance document is to be valid!
====

[cols="2,1,9", options="header"]
.Business Rules for T005, Tender
|===
 | ID | Flag | Rule
 | eSENS-T005-R001 | Fatal | A Tender MUST have a syntax identifier.
 | eSENS-T005-R002 | Fatal | CustomizationID value MUST be 'urn:www.cenbii.eu:{zwsp}transaction:biitrdm090:ver3.0:extended:urn:www.peppol.eu:bis:peppol54a:ver1.0'
 | eSENS-T005-R003 | Fatal | ProfileID value MUST be 'urn:www.cenbii.eu:profile:bii54:ver3.0'
 | eSENS-T005-R004 | Fatal | A Submit Tender Identifier MUST have a schemeURI attribute.
 | eSENS-T005-R005 | Fatal | schemeURI for Submit Tender Identifier MUST be 'urn:uuid'.
 | eSENS-T005-R006 | Fatal | A Submit Tender Identifier MUST be expressed in a UUID syntax (RFC 4122)
 | eSENS-T005-R007 | Fatal | IssueTime MUST have a granularity of seconds
 | eSENS-T005-R008 | Fatal | A Party Identifier MUST have a scheme identifier attribute.
 | eSENS-T005-R009 | Fatal | "A Party Identifier Scheme MUST be from the list of PEPPOL Party Identifiers described in the ""PEPPOL Policy for using Identifiers""."
 | eSENS-T005-R010 | Fatal | An Endpoint Identifier MUST have a scheme identifier attribute.
 | eSENS-T005-R011 | Fatal | "An Endpoint Identifier Scheme MUST be from the list of PEPPOL Party Identifiers described in the ""PEPPOL Policy for using Identifiers""."
 | eSENS-T005-R012 | Fatal | DocumentReference Identifier for a Call for Tender Reference MUST be expressed in a UUID syntax (RFC 4122)
 | eSENS-T005-R013 | Fatal | DocumentReference Identifiers MUST be unique.
 | eSENS-T005-R014 | Fatal | FileName values MUST be unique.
 | eSENS-T005-R015 | Fatal | listID for LocaleCode MUST be 'ISO639-1'.
 | eSENS-T005-R016 | Fatal | LocalCode MUST be a valid Language Code.
 | eSENS-T005-R017 | Fatal | DocumentHash MUST resemble a SHA-256 hash value (32 byte HexString)
 | eSENS-T005-R018 | Fatal | HashAlgorithmMethod MUST be 'http://www.w3.org/2001/04/xmlenc#sha256'
 | eSENS-T005-R019 | Fatal | A Tender MUST identify the Contracting Authority by its party and endpoint identifiers.
 | eSENS-T005-R020 | Fatal | A Tender MUST identify the Economic Operator by its party and endpoint identifiers.
